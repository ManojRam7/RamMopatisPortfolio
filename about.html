About Me

I am a Data Scientist with over 3 years of hands-on experience in data modelling, manipulation, statistical analysis and generating actionable insights across retail, consumer products and insurance domains. I'm currently pursuing a Master's in Data Science at Coventry University, UK with modules covering Big Data Analytics, Machine Learning, Data Management Systems, and Data Visualization.

At Infosys Limited, I've progressed from Associate Data Analyst (Feb 2022 - Dec 2022) to Associate Data Scientist (Dec 2022 - Sep 2023) and Data Scientist (Sep 2023 - May 2025). I've delivered end-to-end advanced analytics and machine learning solutions, transforming raw data into predictive insights, designing interactive Power BI dashboards, building fraud detection models, and orchestrating ML lifecycles using Azure Services. My work spans sales portfolio analytics, marketing mix modeling, and customer segmentation across billion-dollar markets.

I'm proficient in building end-to-end data science pipelines and developing machine learning algorithms with expertise in Python, SQL, PySpark, R, Power BI, Azure Services (Databricks, Data Factory, Data Lake, Azure ML, DevOps), and MLOps practices. Beyond my professional role, I've completed several end-to-end Data Science projects demonstrating real-world applications across multiple domains.

I am passionate about data storytelling—turning complex data into actionable insights that improve business decision-making and business outcomes. I thrive in fast-paced, collaborative environments bridging the gap between data and strategy.

Outside of work, I enjoy exploring new tools, contributing to open-source projects, and continuous learning upskilling in AI/ML and Cloud technologies.
